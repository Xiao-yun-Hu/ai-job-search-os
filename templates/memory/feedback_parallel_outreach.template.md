## Core Principles

- **Parallel, not sequential**: When total target list is small (< 50 companies), do **first round across all targets in parallel**. Don't wait for results before sending the next batch.
- **Maintain daily activity on the main job board**: outbound investments are bi-directional — daily activity also produces *inbound* HR contacts (the platform's algorithmic recommendation system "sees" active resumes).
- **Use first interviews as data, not destination**: First 2-3 interviews are market-research to refine subsequent outreach copy.

## Why

- 50 companies × ~10% cold-outreach reply rate = 5 conversations × turn-into-interview ratio = 2-3 interviews. **Sequential waves mathematically don't reach the decision point in 3-month horizons.**
- Active daily presence yields algorithmic-recommendation inbound contacts. **Stop one day, lose the queue position.**
- Vibe / taste cannot be fully assessed pre-investment. First-wave interviews themselves provide vibe signal. Use it to refine, not to feed back into Match (see Section 4.8 in SYSTEM.md).

## How to apply

1. **Don't suggest reducing daily job-board investments** — keep the rhythm
2. **Don't sequence "first Tier 1, then Tier 2"** — first wave covers all tiers
3. Tier information is for **follow-up priority** (chase Tier 1 non-responses; let Tier 3 lapse)
4. Overseas cold outreach: LinkedIn DM founder/hiring manager, **not** generic portal apply
5. After first 1-2 interviews, write a vibe note + JD-reality note, use to refine future outreach copy
